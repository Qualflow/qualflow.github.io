Automatic Keg Changers With Beer Sell Off

Keep Beer Flowing and Improve Productivity

Automatic Keg Changer improves sales and staff productivity by keeping the beer flowing, allowing uninterrupted service in outlets such as stadiums, large pubs, airports and event venues. Keep your bar staff serving customers by reducing the need for frequent trips to the cellar to change empty kegs.

Qualflow's beer sell off system is now available as an integrated add-on to the Automatic Keg Changers.

    Benefits

    Benefits

    Improve Productivity and sales by serving three times your normal volume without interruption

    Automatic FOB venting eliminates the need to FOB between keg changes

    Optional sell off mode allows staff to sell off beer that is normally wasted due to beer-line cleaning

    Optional large FOB capacity allows operation with fast pour beer systems

    Keg status indicator allows staff to easily identify empty kegs during service and change empty kegs during operation





Specifications

Number of kegs: 1 to 3

Keg inlet: 1/2" or 3/8" push fit

Outlet: 1/2" or 3/8" push fit

FOB vent: 1/2" or 3/8" push fit

Operating voltage: 24V
