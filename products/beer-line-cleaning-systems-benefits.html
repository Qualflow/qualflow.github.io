Core Features & Benefits

Our Beer-Line Cleaning Products Allow You Serve Brewery Fresh Beers of All Shapes and Sizes

1. Better Clean

Compared to other leading automatic beer-line cleaners, Qualflow delivers best in class line hygiene, ensuring you deliver quality fresh beer, customer loyalty and increased sales.

Qualflow’s better cleaning action is delivered through a combination of unique and patent pending technologies including:


  • Proprietary pressure balanced cleaning technology*
  • Advanced cleaning cycles that combine the traditional soak method with detergent refresh and turbulent-flow. This enhances the chemical action of detergents by providing mechanical energy to scrub and lift biofilm, yeast and moulds from the inner surfaces of the beer-line and dispense system

2. Super Capacity Saves Time

Unlike other line cleaning equipment, Qualflow beer-line cleaners do not use mixing tanks. Our dosing technology allows delivery of hundreds of litres of blended detergent on demand.

  • Provides a faster clean by eliminating stop-go operation required to fill tanks
  • Eliminates undercharging and air gaps in beer-lines that cause poor line hygiene
  • Allows effective cleaning of up to 10 (14 with sufficient water supply) beer-lines per cleaning cycle

3. Reduced Beer Loss and Increased Sales

By combining improved line hygiene with Qualflow’s manual or automatic beer sell off systems, you can save thousands by selling the beer in the line before cleaning, while maximizing your sales through improved beer quality.

4.Increased Keg Yields

Our line cleaners are proven to increase keg yields by reducing high beer associated with poor line hygiene.

5. Safer Operation

All our line cleaners help minimize detergent handling to provide safer operation.

6. Rugged Reliable Design

Designed to perform thousands of cleaning cycles, all our line cleaners are robust, low maintenance systems based on our highly reliable portable PLCS line cleaner.


7. Real-Time Monitoring

Optional Bluetooth and Verify-Clean app provides real-time monitoring of beer-line cleaning visits and clean quality data. Our Verify-Clean app is currently validating over 10,000 unique beer-line cleaning visits every month. Real-time data improves beer quality, sales, keg yields and profitability by:

  • Optimising operational efficiencies
  • Standardising and verifying the beer-line cleaning process
  • Monitoring time, date and location of every line cleaned
  • Measuring detergent volume, rinse volume and clean quality score
  • Managing your mobile workforce
